About Dawaai

Pakistan's Leading Digital Healthcare Platform - Bringing Healthcare to Your Doorstep

Who We Are

Dawaai is Pakistan's premier digital healthcare platform, revolutionizing the way people access healthcare services. Founded with a vision to make quality healthcare accessible to everyone, we have grown to become the country's most trusted online pharmacy and healthcare service provider.

As the first-ever internationally certified and registered healthcare merchant in Pakistan (LegitScript Certified), we take pride in our commitment to authenticity, quality, and customer satisfaction. Our platform serves millions of customers across Pakistan, delivering medicines, healthcare products, and services right to their doorstep.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make healthcare accessible, affordable, and convenient for every Pakistani. We believe that quality healthcare should not be a luxury but a fundamental right. Through our innovative digital platform, we bridge the gap between healthcare providers and patients, ensuring that everyone has access to authentic medicines and professional healthcare services.

What We Offer

Dawaai provides a comprehensive range of healthcare services designed to meet all your health needs:

Online Pharmacy

Order authentic medicines online with prescription upload facility. Get the best prices with multiple bank discounts and promotional offers.

Doctor Consultations

Connect with qualified doctors through video consultations. Get expert medical advice from the comfort of your home.

Lab Tests & Checkups

Book lab tests online and get sample collection from your home. Partner labs include Chughtai Labs, Essa Lab, and Pro-Lab.

Home Services

Professional home nursing services, physiotherapy, and medical care at your doorstep.

Health Programs
